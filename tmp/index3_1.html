What to expect once you buy Drift

Our Customer Team has worked with hundreds of customers to help them level up their sales and marketing.

Once you buy Drift, you'll have a dedicated Customer Success Manager and access to our Customer Advocate team. Their job is to make you successful. Below you'll see how they do that.

How much time should you plan to dedicate to getting up and running with Drift? On average, plan to dedicate a few hours in the first 30 days with your Customer Success Manager. And of course, your Customer Success Manager will be available to help you after the first 30 days.

to reach your goals.

Kick off

We get you ready to launch the minute you sign on with us. Before our initial discussion, your Customer Success Manager constructs a success strategy based on your business goals.

Strategy

Launch
